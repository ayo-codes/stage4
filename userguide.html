How To Use Our Goal Tracker

Our how to use guide will teach you how to do the following

  • How to Register
  • How to Login
  • How to Understand the Dashboard Panel
  • How to add a goal
  • How to View your Goals in details and add tasks



How to Register

  1. Click the Register Button on the Home Screen



  2. A Popup window will appear asking you to fill in your details



  3. Fill in the Relevant details as shown below using your own details



  4. Click Register and you will be brought to your personal dashboard. There you have it , you are ready to start recording and smashing your goals.




How to Login

  1. Click the Login button on the Home Page or click Start Now as shown in the photo below



  2. Put in your user details , either your username or email as well as your password and click login. If you forgot your password click forgot your password and an email will be sent to you.



  3. you will be brought to your personal dashboard. There you have it , you are ready to start recording and smashing your goals.




Understanding the Dashboard Panel

  1. When you log into the Dashboard , you will see 3 main metrics
    • Ongoing - This tells you your current goals
    • Completed - This tells you the number of goals you have completed
    • All Goals - This tell you all your goals



  2. The Dashboard also gives you a list of your recent goals.From here,
    • you can see the status of the goal , which can either be inactive or active.
    • You can choose to either View , Edit or Delete the goal.






How to add a goal

  1. To add a new goal from the dashboard , there are two ways to start
    • If you have no goals already , then you can click add a goal
    • If you have some goals already, you can click on My Goals



  2. This will bring you to the My goals panel and from there , click on Add Goal



  3. This will open a popup which allows you to enter the details of your goal . You will be able to enter
    • The name of the goal
    • What the goal is about and give a brief description
    • Set the goal as inactive or an ongoing goal
    • Set a deadline using the calendar



  4. Input the details of your goals , such as the name and the about goal description



  5. You can also use the calendar option to set a deadline for your goal



  6. Now you are set , Click on save changes to save your goal.



  7. Your new goal should now be visible in your my goals panel.






How to View your Goals in details and add tasks

  1. Go to the goal you wish to view and click on the action button and then select view



  2. This will open the goal and give you more information about it . From here you will be able to see the following
    • The total tasks
    • The total completed tasks
    • Your Progress
    • What your goal is about
    • Your due date
    • Your goal status



  3. You can also edit or delete the goal from this panel , using the edit or delete button.



  4. To add a task , click into the “what needs to be done?” box and start typing what task you need to do to help you accomplish your goal and press the enter key and this will add the task to this goal.



    • In this example, I need to meet an architect was the task used, and after the enter key is pressed , it appears in the list of to-dos like it is shown below.




  5. You can also edit or delete your tasks/todos as shown in the example.



  6. To mark a task as done, simply click the checkbox next to the task



  7. This leads to the task being crossed out and also your progress percentage will also change.
